Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

About the app

iZen is a well-being app that displays Dharma quotes — Buddhist teachings and reflections to inspire presence, clarity, and peace in everyday life. Our purpose is simple: to carry these words forward.

Data collected

iZen does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data from its users.

No data collected We do not request your name, email, location, device identifiers, usage data, or any other personal information.

Analytics and tracking

The app does not use analytics tools, third-party tracking SDKs, cookies, or any behavioral monitoring technology.

Advertising

iZen does not display ads and does not share data with advertising networks.

Purchases and payments

If the app offers in-app purchases, all transactions are processed exclusively by Apple via StoreKit. iZen has no access to financial or credit card data.

Compliance with Brazilian LGPD

Since no personal data is collected, iZen operates in full compliance with Brazil's General Data Protection Law (Lei nº 13.709/2018). There is no legal basis for data processing to declare, as no data is processed.

Compliance with Apple guidelines

iZen complies with App Store privacy policies, including App Tracking Transparency (ATT) requirements. The app does not track users.
